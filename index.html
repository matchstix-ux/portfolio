Reviews

I was tasked with conceptualizing and launching a short-form cigar review video and article series. The result was Paulie’s Quick Ash Cigar Reviews—bite-sized videos paired with longer written reviews that gave readers both an overview and deeper dive. I introduced a novel format (“Cheat Sheet” and “Highs, Lows & Whoas”) that broke away from traditional review structures and boosted engagement. The series resonated with both newcomers and seasoned smokers, offering an accessible yet authentic take on premium cigars.

Buying Guides

My work on buying guides focused on helping readers make informed choices in a crowded premium cigar market. From style-specific explorations like Connecticut Shade to trend-focused features such as barrel-aging, these guides balanced education with accessibility. I wrote with both the casual buyer and the aficionado in mind, making complex topics approachable while showcasing expertise. Each guide was designed to drive engagement, foster trust, and encourage readers to explore new cigars with confidence.

Educational

In addition to reviews and guides, I developed educational content that demystified the culture and craft of cigars. Articles like 5 Things About Lanceros or Big Ring Gauge Cigars broke down cigar formats and trends in plain, relatable language. The goal was to engage readers at every level—answering the beginner’s questions while still offering insights for experienced smokers. This body of work helped position the brand as both a trusted teacher and a community voice.
